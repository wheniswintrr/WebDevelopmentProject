Demonstrating Your Process

A process demonstration by dinmoney.

Clients and employers value an artist who can not only produce beautiful artwork but also demonstrate their process. Include thumbnails, rough sketches, and work-in-progress images to show how you approach problem-solving and iterate on designs. This transparency allows potential employers to understand how you think and work, which is often just as important as the final product itself.

Presentation and Consistency

Portfolio belonging to WLOP.

Presentation matters just as much as the content. Ensure your portfolio is clean, well-organized, and easy to navigate. Use a cohesive layout, fonts, and colors to create a professional look. Whether your portfolio is physical or digital, make sure it reflects your attention to detail and commitment to excellence. The way you present your work can impact the impression prospective clients or employers make.
